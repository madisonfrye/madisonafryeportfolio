Overview
Kyros is a multimedia storytelling platform centered around capturing and sharing group experiences. Inspired by the Greek word χρόνος, meaning an opportune moment, Kyros allows experience-seekers to collaboratively create storylines - bullet journals that capture experiences in a multifaceted digital timeline - with their friends/family and share them with inner circles and/or the world. The platform also allows people to use AR to explore their surroundings and discover the best crowd-sourced recommendations sourced from other Kyros users.
Product
Kyros is a multimedia platform to curate, discover, and retrace journeys. Bullet journal with a group of family or friends to document your experiences with video, photos, thoughts, sound, AR, emotion polls, and more. Don't worry about presentation; Kyros takes all of your tidbits to create a short recap video that you can easily show the world. Explore someone else's storyline. Discover your next vacation by viewing user-generated recommendations and media content. Do more than witness their journey; Kyros allows you to retrace their steps.

Our platform centers around three pillars: explore, saved and experiences.
Explore: users can explore the storylines created by their friends/family or public content creators on the platform, allowing them to experience their friends/family's moments, as well as get inspiration for their own plans through public content creators. Explore also crowd-sources the best recommendations for users during their travels.

Saved: users can view a map of storylines sorted by location, to visualize their travel/experience wishlist/itinerary.

Experiences: users can collaboratively create their own storylines, document their shared experiences, toggle between shareability (close friends, friends, public), and plan how to share these moments with their friends, family and the world.

Users
  • Travel influencers, vloggers and content creators seeking to inspire others to recreate their journeys
  • Family and friend circles that want to share their experiences with each other and curate a journey together
  • Solo travelers that want to get personalized recommendations during their trips
