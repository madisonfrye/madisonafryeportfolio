Overview
Last year, the three most common complaints I heard were:

1. tiny (or missing) pant pockets in women's clothing
2. moments of panic when you think your credit card/passport/phone has fallen out or been stolen
3. requiring a huge suitcase filled with an outfit for every occasion “just in case”.

My partner and I wanted to make travel more seamless, starting with clothing. The results showed us people's priorities were surrounding comfort, functionality and style.

Our design philosophy focused on less is more (creating one versatile product) and weighing function and form are equally, so that it was never a trade-off.
Design Process
We surveyed 100+ female travelers and conducted 20+ user interviews to understand travel priorities in clothing.

Then, we designed, prototyped and re-designed (3 iterations) to get to a final product, working with a manufacturing partner to take the pants from concept and pattern to a finished product in 6 months.
Outcome
In the end, we broke even with $0 marketing spend and launched a product that women all over the world say made their travel experience more comfortable and stress-free.
